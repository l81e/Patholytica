About MedBank

A student-built, volunteer-operated academic repository — for present and future generations at MNU.

MedicineBank — known as MedBank — is an independent initiative of a community-based, online studying data repository. It took its full shape during 2023, which corresponds to the second official academic year in MNU's Faculty of Medicine, founded by the freshmen of that year.

The whole purpose of this non-profit project is to facilitate the reach of useful academic materials which are possibly not easily available to everyone. Beyond simply gathering resources, MedBank is dedicated to organising and preserving this data so it may be easily reached at any point in the future — for the upcoming generations of students at MNU.

While whoever contributes to the welfare of the group — whether by providing or referring materials — is mostly a student of MNU's Faculty of Medicine, MedBank is not related to the administration of the faculty, nor is it affiliated with it in any manner. It is fully operated by volunteers.

"Those students of National universities are well competent — of a better academic capacity — and we hope to demonstrate that fact."
— The MedBank Collective
